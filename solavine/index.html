About Solavine

WHERE LIGHT MEETS LIFE — Elevated Care. Meaningful Connection.

Personalized home care designed to help older adults live with comfort, independence, connection, and peace of mind. At Solavine, we believe care is more than assistance with daily tasks. It is companionship, presence, trust, and creating meaningful moments that enrich everyday life.

We partner with families to provide thoughtful, personalized support that allows older adults to continue living safely, comfortably, and confidently in the place they call home.

✨ Solavine was founded in 2026 with a vision to redefine senior care through compassion, dignity, and excellence.

Our Mission

To provide personalized, compassionate care that promotes comfort, independence, connection, and peace of mind.

Our Vision

To become a trusted leader in home care by creating meaningful relationships and delivering exceptional support that enhances quality of life.
